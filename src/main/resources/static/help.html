Version Info

This is RecipeBuddy version 0.0.0.

For updates, please check out our website at https://crazyrecipes.github.io.

Navigating the App

You can navigate with the links in the navbar on the top of the page.
Some content will have links, which will show up as buttons.
You can always hit the back button in your browser to go to the previous page.

Viewing recipes

If you want to view a recipe, just click "View" on its preview card.
This will take you to the recipe viewer, which allows you to see the recipe's name, description, rating, times cooked, ingredients, utensils, steps, tags, and allergens.
You can also set and a timer that will flash the tab and play a sound to notify you when the time is up.
Pro tip: Specify times like 05:00 (MM:SS) or 02:30:00 (HH:MM:SS) in your recipe and the viewer can automatically set the timer to them.

Editing recipes

If you'd like to make changes to your recipe, just click "Edit" on its preview card or "Edit Recipe" at the bottom of the viewer.
The editor lets you save your changes or delete the recipe if you wish.

Searching recipes

The Search page lets you search recipes by keyword. It also lets you filter by ingredients and allergens. You can also tell it to show you recipes that you're only missing a couple ingredients for, or tell it to show you every recipe you have.
